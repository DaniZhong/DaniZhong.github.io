My Journey

Junior full-stack developer

I just graduated from the She Codes Plus Program, where I learned how to design, build and deploy websites using on-trend full-stack technology such as HTML/CSS, Javascript, React.js, Python, Django, and Git. Throughout the program, I developed several end-to-end applications: my portfolio, a news website, and a crowdfunding website, and we finalised the program with a group project. I am now actively looking for a development role.

I hold a bachelor's degree with honours, majoring in Accounting and Application Software. Besides accounting, I studied software development courses, such as C language, data structure, and software engineering/SDLC. I developed several applications as part of the Uni programs using C, FoxPro and Delphi.

After graduation from Uni, I joined Deloitte and became an auditor, then moved on to be a senior consultant at Jade & Company. I gain extensive business knowledge and analytical, problem-solving and stakeholder engagement skills from experiences in consultancy and external auditing for MNCs spanning multiple sectors.

To follow my passion for software development, I joined the SHE CODES PLUS program after taking time to focus on the family. And I now keep self-studying while applying for jobs to work toward being a full-stack developer.
