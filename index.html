My Journey

Junior full-stack developer

I hold a bachelor degree with honor, majored in Accounting and Application Software. Besides of accounting, I studied software development courses including C language, data structure, software engineering/SDLC, etc. and developed several applications as part of the Uni programs using C, FoxPro and Delphi.

After graduation I joined Deloitte and became an auditor, then moved on to be a senior consultant at Jade & Company. I gain extensive business knowledge and experience in consultancy and external auditing for MNCs spanning multiple sectors.

To follow my passion for software development I joined the SHE CODES PLUS program. In this heavily project-based program, I learned HTML/CSS, Python, Django, JavaScript and ReactJS, and completed several end to end applications. I am now working toward to be a full-stack developer.
