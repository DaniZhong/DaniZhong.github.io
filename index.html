My Journey

Graduate full-stack developer

I graduated from the South China University of Technology, majoring in Accounting and Application Software, I studied software development courses including C language, data structure, software engineering concepts, SDLC, etc. I developed several projects as part of the Uni programs. After graduation I joined Deloitte and became an auditor. Then moved on to be a consultant at Jade & Company. To follow my passion for software development I joined the SHE CODES PLUS program. In this program, I learned HTML/CSS, Python, Django, JavaScript and ReactJS, taught and guided by industry mentors. The program is heavily project-based, I finished a project for each modules and just wrapped up our first group project. And I am now working toward to be a full-stack developer.
